# [3.4.0](https://github.com/markoleptic/EncounterPlanner/tree/3.4.0) (2026-03-29)

[Full Changelog](https://github.com/markoleptic/EncounterPlanner/compare/3.3.0...3.4.0)

- Added two new mythic bosses: Vaelgor & Ezzorak and Chimaerus
- Updated heroic timers for Averzian, Vaelgor & Ezzorak, Fallen-King Salhadaar, and Lightblinded Vanguard.
- Added Nullbeam and Shadowmark to Vaelgor & Ezzorak.
- Added Searing Radiance, Tyr's Wrath, and Divine Storm to heroic Lightblinded Vanguard.
- Added Focused Aggression to mythic Vorasius.
- Shortened the duration of Dread Breath and Gloom for improved visibility (Vaelgor & Ezzorak).
- Removed abilities that appeared on PTR but do not appear on live.
